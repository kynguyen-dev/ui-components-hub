## Overview

Cards are the primary grouping surface in modern UIs. The hub includes cards from **shadcn/ui** for general use and **Tremor** for data-focused dashboards.

## shadcn/ui Card

A composable card built from flexible sub-components: `CardHeader`, `CardTitle`, `CardDescription`, `CardContent`, and `CardFooter`.

## Tremor Metric Card

Purpose-built for dashboards. Displays a KPI with optional trend direction and color-coded badges.

<ComponentPreview
  client:load
  title="Metric Card"

## When to Use Which

| Card | Best For | Library |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| shadcn Card | General content, forms, feature lists | shadcn/ui |
| Metric Card | KPIs, dashboards, analytics panels | Tremor |
